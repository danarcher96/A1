Goals

Slammers is local business which runs Volleyball competitions for all ages and averages 100 players per week. To try increase business, the club has decided to have a website made.

The club is expecting to achieve a 50% in average player numbers through the website.

Success Evaluation

To evaluate the success of the website the metric of average players a week will be monitored.

For the site to be deemed successful the club needs to see a 50% increase in average player numbers per week.

Target Audience

The club describes Volleyball as a sport for people of all ages but specified that young families would be the ideal demographic.

With this information in mind the website will be developed to cater for all ages with a focus on young families.

To achieve this the site will be simple to use, pages will have a minimal but practically amount of content on each and the color scheme will be bright and exciting.
